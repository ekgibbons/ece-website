Application to the BME Professional Program

To be considered for status in the BME Professional Program, you must have a GPA of 2.8 in the following courses. You may apply to the Professional Program without completing all the courses, but the grades in the incomplete courses will be counted as C's as placeholder grades. (Please leave those rows blank.) Grades will be verified, and any inaccuracy may disqualify the application.

After you have filled out the form, download the text file and email it to Eric Gibbons (ericgibbons@weber.edu) and Judy Smith (judithsmith@weber.edu).

Pre-Professional GPA:

By submitting this form, you attest that the information is accurate. If it is found that you fabricated data on this form, you will be removed from the program immediately.

  • Grade Values: A=4.00, A-=3.67, B+=3.33, B=3.00, B-=2.67, C+=2.33, C=2.00
  • You may substitute ENGR 1000 or ECE 1000 in lieu of BME 1000.
  • If you have taken MATH 2250 instead of ENGR 2240, please enter the letter grade of that course.
  • If you have taken MATH 2270 and MATH 2280 instead of ENGR 2240, enter the average letter grade of the two courses. If the average is between grades, round up. E.g., if you get an A in MATH 2270 and an A- in MATH 2280, enter an A for MATH 2250.
